About Hulasganj Private ITI

Our story, mission, and commitment to empowering Bihar's youth through quality technical education

Our Story

20+ Years of Shaping Skilled Professionals

Hulasganj Private ITI was established in 2005 with a singular mission — to provide quality vocational education to the youth of Gaya district and surrounding areas of Bihar. Started with just two trade programs, the institute has grown into a fully equipped training center offering 6 NCVT-affiliated trades.

Located in the heart of Hulasganj, our institute has served over 1,200 students who are today working across industries in Bihar, Jharkhand, Delhi, and beyond. We believe that technical education is the most powerful tool for economic upliftment, especially for students from rural and semi-urban backgrounds.

Our institute is affiliated to the National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT), New Delhi, and follows the curriculum laid down by the Directorate General of Training (DGT), Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Government of India.
