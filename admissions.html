Admissions 2025–26

Apply now for the new academic session. Seats are limited — register early to secure your spot.

📋 Eligibility Criteria

  • For 2-Year Trades (Electrician, Fitter): Passed Class 10th (Matric) from a recognized board
  • Age: 14 years minimum at time of admission
  • No upper age limit for ITI admissions
  • Bihar domicile preferred; all states welcome

📄 Required Documents

  • Class 8th / 10th Marksheet & Certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from last school
  • Caste Certificate (for SC/ST/OBC students)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarship applicants)
  • Aadhar Card (Student)
  • Parent/Guardian Aadhar Card
  • Bank Passbook (for scholarship/DBT)
  • 5 recent Passport Size Photographs
  • Domicile Certificate (Bihar)

💰 Fee Structure 2025–26

Trade Duration Annual Fee
Electrician 2 Years ₹15,500/year
Fitter 2 Years ₹15,500/year

* Fees are approximate and subject to revision. SC/ST students may be eligible for full fee waiver under government schemes.

🎓 Scholarships & Fee Concessions

Government scholarships are available for SC/ST/OBC and economically weaker section (EWS) students through the Bihar government's scholarship portal. Our office assists all eligible students in applying. Minority scholarships and NSP (National Scholarship Portal) forms are also available. Contact our office for details.
