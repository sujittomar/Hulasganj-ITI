Our Facilities

Modern infrastructure and well-equipped facilities to support the best learning experience

Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities for Learning

We believe that a good learning environment is as important as a good curriculum.

🔧

Fitter & Mechanic Workshop

Fully equipped workshop with lathes, drilling machines, vices, measuring tools, and precision instruments for hands-on training in fitting operations.

⚡

Electrical Lab

Modern electrical laboratory with wiring panels, transformer units, motor testing setups, MCBs, and safety equipment for electrician trade students.

💻

Computer Lab

Air-conditioned computer lab with 25+ systems, high-speed internet, latest software including Tally, MS Office, and programming environments for COPA students.

🔥

Welding Shop

Dedicated welding area with gas welding, arc welding, and MIG welding setups. Proper ventilation and safety gear provided for all students.

🚗

Motor Vehicle Workshop

Practical training area with actual vehicles, engine components, transmission systems, and diagnostic equipment for MMV trade students.

📚

Library & Reading Room

A quiet reading space with trade-specific reference books, NCVT syllabus materials, previous year papers, and general knowledge resources.

🏫

Classrooms

Bright, spacious classrooms with blackboards, charts, and learning aids. Each classroom is designed for focused learning and comfortable seating.

🏃

Sports Ground

Open grounds for cricket, football, and athletics. Regular sports activities are organized to promote physical fitness and team spirit among students.

🏥

First Aid Room

A first aid room with basic medical supplies and trained staff ensures student safety during workshop and lab activities at the institute.
