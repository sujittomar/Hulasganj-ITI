Our Trade Courses

NCVT-affiliated vocational training programs designed for industry readiness and employment

Choose Your Trade

Explore All Trade Programs

All courses are affiliated to NCVT, New Delhi and follow the curriculum set by DGT, Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Government of India.

⚡Electrician

Electrician

Duration: 2 Years Eligibility: 10th Pass NCVT Certified

The Electrician trade trains students in the installation, maintenance, and repair of electrical systems in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. Students gain hands-on experience with wiring, switchgear, motors, and safety protocols.

Career Opportunities: Electrical Technician, Wireman, Industrial Electrician, Power Plant Worker, Self Employment

  • Domestic & industrial wiring
  • Electrical machine maintenance
  • Motor rewinding & control panels
  • Safety standards & regulations
  • Renewable energy basics (solar)
🔧Fitter

Fitter

Duration: 2 Years Eligibility: 10th Pass NCVT Certified

The Fitter trade is one of the most sought-after trades in Indian manufacturing. Students learn to assemble, fit, and maintain mechanical components used in machinery, pipelines, and structural fabrication.

Career Opportunities: Fitter in Railways, BHEL, NTPC, Defense PSUs, Manufacturing Industries, Apprenticeships

  • Bench work & fitting operations
  • Lathe & drilling machine operations
  • Blueprint reading & measurement
  • Pipe fitting & assembly
  • Hydraulic & pneumatic systems
💻COPA

Computer Operator & Programming Assistant (COPA)

Duration: 1 Year Eligibility: 10th Pass NCVT Certified

COPA prepares students for roles in the IT sector and office environments. It covers computer fundamentals, office applications, programming basics, and internet technologies. Ideal for students interested in digital careers.

Career Opportunities: Data Entry Operator, Computer Operator, Office Assistant, BPO Jobs, Tally Operator

  • MS Office & Google Workspace
  • Basic programming (Python/HTML)
  • Tally ERP & accounting software
  • Internet & email management
  • Database management basics
🔥Welder

Welder

Duration: 1 Year Eligibility: 8th Pass NCVT Certified

The Welder trade focuses on gas welding, arc welding, and other modern welding techniques. Welders are in high demand in the construction, shipbuilding, automotive, and fabrication industries.

Career Opportunities: Welder in Construction, Fabrication Units, Shipyards, Railways, Defense

  • Gas welding & cutting
  • Arc & MIG/TIG welding
  • Welding symbols & blueprints
  • Metal joining & fabrication
  • Safety in welding operations
🚗MMV

Mechanic Motor Vehicle (MMV)

Duration: 2 Years Eligibility: 10th Pass NCVT Certified

This trade covers the servicing, overhauling, and repair of automobiles including cars, trucks, and buses. With the growing automobile sector in India, MMV graduates are in high demand.

Career Opportunities: Automobile Service Technician, Mechanic at Service Centers, Transport Companies, Self-Employment (garage)

  • Engine overhauling & servicing
  • Transmission & braking systems
  • Electrical systems in vehicles
  • Fuel injection & emission systems
  • Modern hybrid/EV vehicle basics
🪠Plumber

Plumber

Duration: 1 Year Eligibility: 8th Pass NCVT Certified

Plumber trade students learn installation and maintenance of water supply systems, sanitary fittings, and drainage networks in buildings. With India's housing boom, skilled plumbers are always in demand.

Career Opportunities: Plumber in Construction Projects, Municipal Corporations, Housing Societies, Self-Employment

  • Pipe fitting & installation
  • Drainage & sewage systems
  • Sanitary ware installation
  • Water supply systems
  • Blueprint reading for plumbing
