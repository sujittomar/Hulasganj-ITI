Who We Are

Building Bihar's Technical Workforce

Hulasganj Private ITI is a government-affiliated Industrial Training Institute located in Hulasganj, Jehanabad district, Bihar. We offer NCVT-recognized trade programs designed to equip students with practical skills that meet modern industry standards.

Our vision is to make quality vocational education accessible to every youth — especially from rural Bihar — and connect them with employment opportunities across India.
